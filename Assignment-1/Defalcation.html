Defalcation

Meaning:
The act of secretly taking money that is in your care or that belongs to an organization or business you work for

Usage in a sentence:

  • “There are charges against them of defalcation and mismanagement. ”
  • “ We referred earlier to the very high incidence of defalcations by sole practitioners.”
